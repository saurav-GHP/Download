About The Game

TPS Demo Game is a third-person shooter prototype developed using the Godot Engine. The project explores futuristic environments, advanced robotics, and space-themed gameplay mechanics. Players control a character from a third-person perspective while exploring high-tech facilities and spaceship interiors in a sci-fi universe.

The demo focuses on testing gameplay mechanics such as player movement, camera control, exploration systems, and experimental combat mechanics. The game world is designed with a cyber-futuristic style inspired by advanced technology and deep-space environments.

This early version demonstrates the foundation of the game. Future updates will introduce improved graphics, expanded maps, enemy AI systems, new weapons, and more advanced missions that expand the gameplay experience.

The project is developed by Team Saurav as an experimental learning project focused on game design, programming, and creative world building.
