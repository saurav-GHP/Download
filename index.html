About The Game

TPS Demo Game is a third-person shooter prototype developed using the Godot Engine. The project explores futuristic environments including robotics laboratories and space technology facilities.

Players explore spaceship interiors and research complexes while controlling a character in third-person view.

The game is currently in early development and will receive future updates including new maps, AI enemies, and improved graphics.

Developed by Team Saurav as a learning project in programming and game development.
